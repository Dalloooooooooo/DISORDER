The video art works

Five works tracing five lived experiences of Anxiety Disorder through
the audiovisual language of video art—expressive, emotional, synesthetic, metaphorical. Each story is shaped through in-depth conversations with individuals who opened up about what an anxiety attack feels like,
in their own words.
Together, these expressive video art works form a collective portrait
of experiences that often remain hidden—lived realities of anxiety that are routinely minimized, misinterpreted, or dismissed as exaggeration, reclaiming instead their depth, complexity, and legitimacy.
